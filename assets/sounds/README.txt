# Sound Effects for Daily Love Chest

Add the following sound files to this directory:

1. `chest-open.mp3` - A gentle wooden chest opening sound
2. `sparkle.mp3` - A magical twinkling sound when sparkles appear
3. `message.mp3` - A soft notification sound when a regular message appears
4. `special.mp3` - A more celebratory sound when a special message appears

Recommended formats: MP3 files, duration 1-3 seconds, volume normalized.

You can find free sound effects on websites like:
- https://freesound.org/
- https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
- https://elements.envato.com/sound-effects/free
